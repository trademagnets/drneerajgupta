Meet Dr. Neeraj Gupta

✨ I’m Dr. Neeraj Gupta

from the foothills of the Himalayas. Despite a successful medical career, I felt unfulfilled—prescriptions only provided temporary relief, not true healing. The question “How can I help people truly heal?” grew louder until I could no longer ignore it. I left my practice to seek a deeper solution.

Under my enlightened Master, Dr. Ajay Kotwal, I discovered Spiritual Enlightenment, Mindfulness, Meditation, and Cosmic Consciousness. He taught me: Consciousness is everywhere; you are an ocean of Consciousness.

Blending ancient wisdom with modern psychology, I developed holistic tools for healing. Now, I guide individuals to overcome suffering, find peace, and build resilience. Leaving medicine wasn’t easy, but it was right. Today, I don’t just treat symptoms—I empower people to heal from within and transform their lives.

"Healing begins the moment you decide to listen to your soul." – Dr. Neeraj

Curious mind?

I'm new to spirituality — where do I start? ▼
You start exactly where you are. Dr. Neeraj offers a discovery call to understand your journey and suggest the best path forward — meditation, mentorship, or energy healing.
Do you offer online sessions? ▼
Yes, all sessions are available via Zoom or phone. Healing transcends distance — you can join from anywhere in the world.
How is this different from regular therapy? ▼
Dr. Neeraj blends modern psychological understanding with timeless spiritual wisdom. You’ll receive tools for deep inner transformation, not just symptom management.
What can I expect in a discovery call? ▼
A compassionate 30-minute conversation to understand your needs, explain how spiritual healing works, and answer any questions — no pressure, purely supportive.
